Self-Assessment: How Adaptable Am I?

Rate yourself from 1 (rarely) to 5 (consistently)

1. I approach new situations with curiosity rather than anxiety.

2. I actively seek feedback on my performance, including critical feedback.

3. When I encounter a new system or process, I focus on learning it rather than criticising it.

4. I voice concerns about change through constructive channels rather than informal complaint.

5. I recover quickly from professional setbacks and maintain my performance.

6. I proactively support colleagues who are struggling with change.

7. I can tolerate uncertainty and maintain effectiveness while outcomes are unclear.
